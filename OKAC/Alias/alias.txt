.mayday The VATSIM CoC (B6) states that air traffic control requests the pilot to terminate the emergency, then the pilot shall do so IMMEDIATELY or disconnect from the network.
.runway The VATSIM CoC (B1) states that a pilot shall not connect to the VATSIM network on a runway. Please disconnect immediately and re-connect at an open stand.
.helpvacate You have not properly vacated the runway. After landing, you have to continue taxiing off the runway and past the hold short line until your whole aircraft is clear. If you stop before this point, you are considered still on the runway and can cause significant delays and issues for ATC and other pilots. If you are unable to follow further instructions, a supervisor will be called.
.helphs You have not correctly followed a hold short instruction. When instructed to hold short, you are required to hold before that position ensuring that there is enough space for other aircraft to pass on that taxiway. Taxiing beyond your clearance limit can cause significant delays and issues for ATC and pilots. If you are unable to follow further instructions, a supervisor will be called.
.warn As per VATSIM CoC (B8(c)), pilots are expected to promptly comply with basic air traffic control instructions that are applicable to their flight rules. This includes instructions such as holding; flying a speed, heading or flight level; approach to land; correctly fly a cleared routing; fly a holding patter. It appears that you are unable to comply with these instructions. If you do not comply with further instructions a supervisor will be called.
.textpilot As per VATSIM CoC (A14(a)), pilots should be able to at a minimum receive voice, but may send messages via text. If you are unable to comply you MUST disconnect from the network otherwise a supervisor will be called.
